Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 19, 2026

At Scan Aushadhi, privacy is our priority. We don't want your data; we just want to help you understand medicines safely.

Disclaimer: This app is strictly for informational purposes and NOT a substitute for professional medical advice.

1. Zero Data Logging

  • No Accounts: No signup or login is ever required.
  • No PII: We do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers.
  • Temporary Photos: Scanned images are processed in-memory and deleted instantly.

2. Essential Permissions

We only request Camera access to scan medicine labels. We cannot and do not access your private gallery or files without your direct action.

3. Secure AI Processing

We use encrypted AI APIs to read medicine text. This process is fully anonymous. No personal metadata is ever shared with third parties.

4. Maximum Security

Your session is protected with industry-standard encryption. Since we do not maintain a user database, there is no risk of your personal data being leaked.
